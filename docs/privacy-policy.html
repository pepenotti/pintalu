Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Pintalú is a drawing app for kids. This policy explains how the app handles data. The short version: we collect nothing.

Data collection

Pintalú does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no network requests.

Local storage

All drawings and app settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

Pintalú may request access to your device's media library solely to save drawings to your photo gallery when you choose to export them. No other permissions are used.

Children's privacy

Pintalú is designed for children. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it fully complies with children's privacy laws including COPPA and GDPR-K.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
